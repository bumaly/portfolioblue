Neon Tokyo Layouts

UI/UX DESIGN 2026

An exploration of high-contrast brutalist design principles merged with functional, modern web applications. The goal was to break away from soft shadows and rounded corners, bringing back the raw, unpolished energy of early internet aesthetics while maintaining excellent usability.

This design option heavily relies on Space Grotesk for bold, opinionated headlines, paired with IBM Plex Mono for technical and navigational elements. The result is a highly legible yet distinctly stylized interface that stands out in a sea of minimalist corporate designs.
