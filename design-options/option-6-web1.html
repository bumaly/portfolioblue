Cybernetic Dreams

[ Category: Web Design ] [ Year: 2026 ]

Welcome to my latest project page! This design pays homage to the early days of the World Wide Web, often referred to as Web 1.0 or the GeoCities era. It uses classic elements like Times New Roman, distinct bright blue hyperlinks, and rigid frame-like layouts.

[ Image: cyber_dreams_preview.jpg (54KB) ]

By retaining the 3-column layout but styling it with CSS ridge borders and dotted dividers, we maintain your core structure while fully embracing the nostalgic, unpolished charm of the 90s internet.
